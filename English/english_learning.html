英语学习资料 - 分类汇总

一、序数词 (Ordinal Numbers)

  • 1st first (第一) Example: Today is the 1st day of the month. (今天是这个月的第一天)
  • 2nd second (第二) Example: He is the 2nd person to arrive. (他是第二个到的人)
  • 3rd third (第三) Example: March is the 3rd month of the year. (三月是一年中的第三个月)
  • 4th fourth (第四) Example: Thursday is the 4th day of the week. (周四是一周的第四天)
  • 5th fifth (第五) Example: May is the 5th month of the year. (五月是一年中的第五个月)
  • 10th tenth (第十) Example: October is the 10th month of the year. (十月是一年中的第十个月)
  • 20th twentieth (第二十) Example: This is my 20th year of working. (这是我工作的第二十个年头)
  • 21st twenty-first (第二十一) Example: The 21st of June is Children's Day. (6月21日是儿童节)
  • 100th hundredth (第一百) Example: This is the 100th task we completed. (这是我们完成的第一百个任务)

二、时间相关 (Time Related)

2.1 基础时间单位

  • second 秒 Example: The task takes 10 seconds to finish. (这个任务需要10秒完成)
  • minute 分钟 Example: Please wait for 5 minutes. (请等待5分钟)
  • hour 小时 Example: I work 8 hours a day. (我一天工作8小时)
  • day 天 Example: We need 3 days to complete the project. (我们需要3天完成这个项目)
  • week 周 (wk) Example: We have a meeting once a week. (我们每周开一次会)
  • month 月 (mo) Example: The report is due next month. (这份报告下个月截止)
  • year 年 (yr) Example: The company was founded in 2020. (这家公司成立于2020年)

2.2 星期 (带缩写)

  • Monday 周一 (Mon.) Example: The weekly meeting is on Monday. (周会在周一召开)
  • Tuesday 周二 (Tue./Tues.) Example: I have a training on Tuesday. (我周二有培训)
  • Wednesday 周三 (Wed.) Example: We finish the task on Wednesday. (我们周三完成任务)
  • Thursday 周四 (Thu./Thur.) Example: The deadline is Thursday afternoon. (截止日期是周四下午)
  • Friday 周五 (Fri.) Example: We have a team building on Friday. (我们周五有团建)
  • Saturday 周六 (Sat.) Example: I rest on Saturday. (我周六休息)
  • Sunday 周日 (Sun.) Example: The system maintenance is on Sunday. (系统维护在周日)

2.3 月份 (带缩写)

  • January 一月 (Jan.) Example: New Year is in January. (元旦在一月)
  • February 二月 (Feb.) Example: Valentine's Day is in February. (情人节在二月)
  • March 三月 (Mar.) Example: Women's Day is on March 8th. (妇女节在3月8日)
  • April 四月 (Apr.) Example: April Fool's Day is on April 1st. (愚人节在4月1日)
  • May 五月 (May) Example: Labor Day is on May 1st. (劳动节在5月1日)
  • June 六月 (Jun.) Example: Children's Day is on June 1st. (儿童节在6月1日)
  • July 七月 (Jul.) Example: It's very hot in July. (七月非常热)
  • August 八月 (Aug.) Example: We have a holiday in August. (我们八月有假期)
  • September 九月 (Sep./Sept.) Example: Teachers' Day is on September 10th. (教师节在9月10日)
  • October 十月 (Oct.) Example: National Day is on October 1st. (国庆节在10月1日)
  • November 十一月 (Nov.) Example: We start the new project in November. (我们十一月启动新项目)
  • December 十二月 (Dec.) Example: Christmas is in December. (圣诞节在十二月)

2.4 四季

  • spring 春天 Example: The weather is warm in spring. (春天天气温暖)
  • summer 夏天 Example: We often go swimming in summer. (我们夏天经常去游泳)
  • autumn/fall 秋天 Example: The leaves turn yellow in autumn. (秋天树叶变黄)
  • winter 冬天 Example: It snows a lot in winter in the north. (北方冬天经常下雪)

三、计量单位 (Units of Measurement)

3.1 基础物理单位

  • meter 米 (m) Example: The room is 5 meters long. (这个房间长5米)
  • kilometer 千米/公里 (km) Example: The distance is 10 kilometers. (距离是10公里)
  • centimeter 厘米 (cm) Example: The book is 20 centimeters wide. (这本书宽20厘米)
  • kilogram 千克/公斤 (kg) Example: The box weighs 2 kilograms. (这个箱子重2公斤)
  • gram 克 (g) Example: The sugar is 500 grams. (这袋糖500克)
  • liter 升 (L) Example: Please pour 1 liter of water. (请倒1升水)
  • milliliter 毫升 (mL) Example: The bottle has 500 milliliters of milk. (这个瓶子有500毫升牛奶)
  • degree Celsius 摄氏度 (°C) Example: The temperature is 25 degrees Celsius. (温度是25摄氏度)

3.2 计算机存储单位

  • bit 比特 Example: A byte consists of 8 bits. (一个字节由8个比特组成)
  • byte 字节 (B) Example: The text file is 1000 bytes. (这个文本文件1000字节)
  • kilobyte 千字节 (KB) Example: The picture is 500 KB in size. (这张图片大小是500千字节)
  • megabyte 兆字节 (MB) Example: The video is 100 MB. (这个视频100兆字节)
  • gigabyte 吉字节 (GB) Example: The hard disk has 1000 GB of storage. (这个硬盘有1000吉字节存储空间)
  • terabyte 太字节 (TB) Example: The server has 10 TB of data. (这个服务器有10太字节的数据)
  • petabyte 拍字节 (PB) Example: The data center stores 100 PB of data. (这个数据中心存储了100拍字节的数据)

四、天气相关 (Weather Related)

  • sunny 晴朗的 Example: It's sunny today. (今天天气晴朗)
  • cloudy 多云的 Example: The weather is cloudy tomorrow. (明天多云)
  • rainy 下雨的 Example: It will be rainy this afternoon. (今天下午会下雨)
  • snowy 下雪的 Example: It's snowy in the north today. (今天北方下雪)
  • windy 刮风的 Example: It's windy outside. (外面在刮风)
  • foggy 有雾的 Example: It's foggy in the morning. (早上有雾)
  • hot 热的 Example: It's very hot in summer. (夏天非常热)
  • cold 冷的 Example: It's cold in winter. (冬天很冷)
  • warm 温暖的 Example: The weather is warm in spring. (春天天气温暖)
  • cool 凉爽的 Example: It's cool in autumn. (秋天很凉爽)
  • temperature 温度 Example: The temperature is 20 degrees today. (今天温度20度)

五、交通工具 (Transportation)

  • car 汽车 Example: I go to work by car. (我开车上班)
  • bus 公交车 Example: He takes the bus to the office. (他坐公交去办公室)
  • subway 地铁 Example: The subway is fast and convenient. (地铁又快又方便)
  • train 火车 Example: I take the train to travel. (我坐火车旅行)
  • plane 飞机 Example: We go to Beijing by plane. (我们坐飞机去北京)
  • bicycle/bike 自行车 Example: I ride a bike to the park. (我骑自行车去公园)
  • taxi 出租车 Example: Take a taxi to the airport. (坐出租车去机场)
  • ship 轮船 Example: The ship sails to the island. (轮船驶向那个岛屿)
  • motorcycle 摩托车 Example: He rides a motorcycle to work. (他骑摩托车上班)

六、办公用品 (Office Supplies)

  • computer 电脑 Example: I use a computer to work. (我用电脑工作)
  • laptop 笔记本电脑 Example: I take my laptop to the meeting. (我带笔记本电脑去开会)
  • monitor 显示器 Example: The monitor is 27 inches. (这个显示器27英寸)
  • keyboard 键盘 Example: The keyboard is wireless. (这个键盘是无线的)
  • mouse 鼠标 Example: Please pass me the mouse. (请把鼠标递给我)
  • printer 打印机 Example: Print the document with the printer. (用打印机打印这份文档)
  • paper 纸张 Example: We need more A4 paper. (我们需要更多A4纸)
  • pen 钢笔 Example: Sign the document with a pen. (用钢笔签署这份文档)
  • pencil 铅笔 Example: Use a pencil to take notes. (用铅笔做笔记)
  • notebook 笔记本 Example: Write down the meeting minutes in the notebook. (在笔记本上记录会议纪要)
  • folder 文件夹 Example: Put the documents in the folder. (把文档放进文件夹)
  • stapler 订书机 Example: Staple the papers with a stapler. (用订书机把纸张订起来)

七、会议相关 (Meeting Related)

  • meeting 会议 Example: We have a daily meeting at 9 AM. (我们上午9点开日常会议)
  • conference 研讨会/大型会议 Example: Attend the annual conference next week. (下周参加年度研讨会)
  • agenda 议程 Example: Please send me the meeting agenda. (请把会议议程发给我)
  • minutes 会议纪要 Example: Write the meeting minutes after the meeting. (会议结束后写会议纪要)
  • participant 参会者 Example: All participants should arrive on time. (所有参会者需准时到达)
  • host 主持人/主办方 Example: He is the host of the meeting. (他是这次会议的主持人)
  • speaker 发言人 Example: The speaker will talk about the new project. (发言人将介绍新项目)
  • deadline 截止日期 Example: The deadline for the report is Friday. (报告的截止日期是周五)
  • decision 决议 Example: We make a decision in the meeting. (我们在会议上做出决议)
  • action item 行动项 Example: Record the action items in the minutes. (把行动项记录在纪要里)
  • postpone 推迟 Example: We need to postpone the meeting to next week. (我们需要把会议推迟到下周)
  • cancel 取消 Example: Cancel the meeting due to bad weather. (因天气不好取消会议)
